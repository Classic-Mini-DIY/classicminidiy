A New Take on a Classic Car

Keeping the Classic Mini alive

Classic Mini DIY was created as a simple youtube channel to help other classic Mini owners work on their classic cars. As time progressed we realized that we could provide the classic Mini community with the largest resource for litrature and how-to guides for working on their old cars. Whether you need technical specifications for your car, or are looking for details on color codes Classic Mini DIY is your source.

Historic Literature

Added on a regular basis, we offer access to original documentation and literature on the classic Mini

Technical Specifications

Sourced directly from Haynes Manuals, Classic Mini DIY offers in depth details on torque specs, clearances, and much more.

How-to Guides

Detailed and comprehensive how to videos and guides showing exactly how to perform common and sometimes less common jobs on your classic mini.
